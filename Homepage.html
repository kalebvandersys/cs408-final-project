The Apartments:

Discover a new frontier of comfort and innovation at Other-Worldly Apartments, the first residential community built on the serene surface of the Moon. Designed for modern explorers, dreamers, and pioneers, these state-of-the-art units combine breathtaking views with cutting-edge technology for a truly out-of-this-world living experience. Wake up each morning to the gentle glow of Earthrise through panoramic crystal-glass windows engineered to filter cosmic light. Inside, enjoy gravity-optimized furnishings, smart-habitation climate control, and hydroponic greenery that brings a touch of Earth’s life to your lunar home. Residents have access to premium amenities including a zero-gravity fitness center, crater-side walking trails, a domed observation lounge, high-speed interstellar communication, and a secure underground transport hub connecting directly to Lunar Base Alpha. Whether you’re seeking a peaceful escape, a scientific retreat, or a glimpse of humanity’s future, Other-Worldly Apartments offers unmatched tranquility, exclusivity, and cosmic beauty—luxury living just a quarter-million miles from home.

Property Manager: Orion Voss

Bio: Orion Voss brings a decade of interplanetary property management experience to Other-Worldly Apartments, where he leads resident services with a steady hand and a cosmic sense of hospitality. With a background in lunar infrastructure and habitat sustainability, Orion ensures every unit remains safe, comfortable, and perfectly calibrated for low-gravity living. Known for his approachable demeanor and quick problem-solving skills, he’s the go-to guide for everything from air-filtration tuning to arranging scenic crater-rim tours. Orion is dedicated to making Other-Worldly Apartments feel like home—no matter how far from Earth you are.
